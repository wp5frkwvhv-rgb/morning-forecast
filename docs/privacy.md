# Privacy Policy — Morning Forecast Texter

**Last updated:** March 2026

## Overview
Morning Forecast Texter is a personal-use SMS notification service that sends daily weather forecasts to the account owner.

## Data Collection
This service collects and uses only the following data:
- **Phone number:** Your mobile phone number, used solely to deliver the daily weather SMS.
- **Location data:** A fixed latitude/longitude (McKinney, TX) used to fetch weather forecasts. No GPS or real-time location tracking is performed.

## Data Sharing
- We do **not** sell, share, or distribute any personal data to third parties.
- Weather data is fetched from Open-Meteo (public API, no personal data sent).
- SMS delivery is handled by Twilio. Twilio's privacy policy applies to message transmission.

## Data Storage
- No personal data is stored in databases or logs beyond what is required for SMS delivery.
- Twilio may retain message logs per their data retention policy.

## Opt-Out
To stop receiving messages, disable the scheduled task or remove your phone number from the configuration.

## Contact
For questions about this privacy policy, contact the service owner directly.
