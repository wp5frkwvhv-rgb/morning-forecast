Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Morning Forecast Texter is a personal-use SMS notification service that sends daily weather forecasts to opted-in subscribers.

Data Collection

This service collects and uses only the following data:

  • Phone number: Your mobile phone number, used solely to deliver the daily weather SMS.
  • Location data: A fixed latitude/longitude (McKinney, TX) used to fetch weather forecasts. No GPS or real-time location tracking is performed.

Data Sharing

  • We do not sell, share, or distribute any personal data to third parties.
  • Weather data is fetched from Open-Meteo (public API, no personal data sent).
  • SMS delivery is handled by Twilio. Twilio privacy policy applies to message transmission.

Data Storage

No personal data is stored in databases or logs beyond what is required for SMS delivery. Twilio may retain message logs per their data retention policy.

Opt-Out

To stop receiving messages at any time, reply STOP to any message. You can also reply HELP for assistance. Message and data rates may apply.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact the service owner directly.
