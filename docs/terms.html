Service Description

Morning Forecast Texter is a personal-use automated SMS service that delivers daily weather and pollen forecast notifications to opted-in subscribers.

Messaging Terms

Frequency: One (1) SMS message per day, typically sent at approximately 6:30 AM CST.

Content: Weather forecast data for McKinney, TX including temperature, conditions, precipitation probability, and optional pollen levels.

Opt-out: You may stop receiving messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for assistance.

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.
